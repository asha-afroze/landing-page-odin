My TOP 4 Favorite Ghibli Films

Howl and Sophie, one of my favorite ghibli couples! I love their dynamic and they are tooo cute. From one of my most favorite films - Howl's Moving Castle!
Chihiro (the little girl) and No Face (the ghost) from Spirited Away! A story about a young girl who enters a magical world of spirits to save her parents from a witch's curse.
Another really cute ghibli movie - Ponyo. A story about a magical fish who ends up on the surface in the little green pail of a boy named Sosuke.
Last but not least, Arriety! This film is so aesthetically pleasing. It's about Arrietty who's a tiny girl and a 'Borrower'. She secretly lives beneath a human house and meets Sho (the boy).
"A heart's a heavy burden."
